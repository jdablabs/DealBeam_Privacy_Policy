# Privacy Policy for DealBeam

**Last Updated: February 16, 2026**

jdablabs LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the DealBeam mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Barcode Scan Data**: When you scan a product barcode, we process the barcode number to look up product information and prices. Scanned product data is stored locally on your device.
- **Device Information**: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and diagnostics.

### Camera Access
- The App requires camera access solely to scan product barcodes. We do not capture, store, or transmit photos or video. The camera is used only for real-time barcode detection.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Authenticate your identity and manage your account
- Look up product information from scanned barcodes
- Compare prices across retailers (Google Shopping, Walmart, eBay, Amazon)
- Provide AI-powered product recommendations and alternatives
- Store your favorites and recent scans locally on your device
- Improve and optimize the App

The App contains affiliate links to Amazon.com through the Amazon Associates Program. When you click an Amazon link in the App and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. These links include our affiliate tag to track referrals.

- **Cloud Processing**: Barcode lookups, price comparisons, and AI recommendations are processed through our secure Firebase Cloud Functions. Your API requests are proxied through our servers â€” no API keys are stored on your device.

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained as long as you maintain an active account.
- Locally stored data (favorites, recent scans) remains on your device until you clear the App data or uninstall the App.
- You may delete your account at any time by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.
